George Bush Sr. on Principles & Values   Click here for 17 full quotes on Principles & Values OR other political leaders on Principles & Values. 1970: Ran for Senate unsuccessfully a second time. (Nov 2010) 1988 cover of Newsweek: "Fighting the Wimp Factor". (Nov 2010) 1988 OpEd: "Born with a silver foot in his mouth". (Mar 2010) 1973: Heal rift after Karl Rove elected as College GOP Chair. (Mar 2010) 1974: Considered as Ford VP; 1976: formed presidential PAC. (Mar 2010) 1973: Appointed Karl Rove as chair of College Republicans. (Mar 2010) 1976: Considered for V.P. by Ford. (Mar 2010) Requires that all hotels have exercise bikes in his room. (Jun 2009) Pardoned 6 officials in Iran-Contra scandal. (Jun 2007) 1953: Daughter Robin died of childhood cancer. (Sep 2004) 1992: Saddam foiled in plotting his assassination in Kuwait. (Jun 2004) 1992 debate: Caught glancing at wristwatch. (Feb 2003) It’s an emotional roller-coaster; Gore’s a sore loser. (Jan 1989) Free markets, free speech, free elections.
