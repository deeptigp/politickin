Government is responsible for abuses in private prisons. (Jul 2009) Favors defendants’ rights. (Dec 2000) Fleeing police is not in itself sufficient to allow search. (Jan 2000) No death penalty without considering mitigating evidence. (Apr 2000) Can't lengthen prison sentences retroactively. (Mar 2000) Life-sentence alternative should affect death sentence. (Feb 2011) Congress, not EPA, determines when cost-benefit applies. (Jul 2009) Migratory birds should extend CWA to isolated waters. (Jan 2001) Eminent domain applies to public economic development. (Jun 2005) Clean Water Act applies to tributaries & wetlands. (Jun 2006) States can request that EPA regulate CO2 greenhouse gases. (Apr 2007) OK to sue polluters for past pollution. (Jan 2000) Extend CWA restrictions to isolated water bodies. (Jan 2001)   John Paul Stevens on Families & Children   Click here for 2 full quotes on Families & Children OR background on Families & Children. (May 2000)   John Paul Stevens on Foreign Policy   Click here for  the full quote on Foreign Policy OR background on Foreign Policy. UN Charter doesn't require heeding International Court. (Jun 2001) States have authority to protect patient rights. (Jun 2002) States decide insurance issues, not federal government.
